# AutoBookr Pilot Customer Outreach Plan

## 🎯 Target Trades (5-10)

1. **Plumbers** – High call volume, appointment-heavy, often booking-averse
2. **Electricians** – Similar to plumbers, time-sensitive scheduling
3. **Gas Safe Engineers** – Regulated, need punctuality, good fit for booking reliability
4. **Roofters / Roofers** – Seasonal work, need clear scheduling for site visits
5. **Handymen / General Builders** – Smaller jobs, recurring appointments
6. **Domestic Cleaners** – Repeat bookings, great for recurring schedules
7. **Landscapers / Gardeners** – Weather permitting, need flexible booking
8. **Painters & Decorators** – Day-long jobs, need clear time blocks
9. **HVAC Engineers** – Commercial/residential, appointment-driven
10. **Carpet/Flooring Fitters** – Measurement visits + fitting appointments

---

## 📧 Cold Message Template

**Subject:** Free tool to automate your job bookings – no more phone tag

Hi [Name],

I'm building **AutoBookr** – a simple tool that lets customers book your services online, 24/7, without you chasing calls or playing phone tag.

I'd love to offer you a **free pilot** – no cost, no commitment. Just want to see if it helps reduce the back-and-forth on booking jobs.

If you're interested, let me know – can set it up in about 15 minutes.

Cheers,
[Your Name]

## 🔍 Where to Find These Businesses

### Local Facebook Groups
- Search: "[Your Town] Traders" / "[Area] Local Tradesmen" / "Small Businesses [County]"
- Join local "Buy, Sell, Trade" groups – often have tradespeople promoting services
- Search: "Electricians in [Town]"

### Online Directories
- **Checkatrade** – Browse trades, reach out to those with few/no reviews (eager for leads)
- **TrustATrader** – Similar approach
- **Rated People** – Active traders looking for work
- **Yell.com** – Local business listings

### Nextdoor
- Post in your local area offering free pilot
- Respond to posts asking for recommendations (tradespeople often engage)

### Local Networking
- Chamber of Commerce events
- Trade association meetups (e.g., SNIPEF for plumbers, SELECT for electricians)
- Local business Facebook groups

### Direct Approach
- Google Maps search "[trade] near me" – get names, find them on Facebook/email

## 🔄 Follow-Up Sequence

### Day 0: Initial Message
- Send cold message via FB/Email/Nextdoor

### Day 3: Follow-Up #1
> "Hi [Name], just checking if you saw my message about AutoBookr? Happy to answer any questions."

### Day 7: Follow-Up #2
> "No worries if not interested – just wanted to mention it's completely free for the pilot. Let me know if you'd like to give it a try!"

### Day 14: Final Attempt
> "One last nudge – I'd love to get your feedback on AutoBookr. If now's not a good time, let me know and I can check back in a month?"

### After 3 Unresponses: Move to "Cold" list for future outreach

---

## ✅ Quick Wins Tips

- Lead with **"free"** and **"no commitment"** – lowers resistance
- Keep messages short – busy tradespeople won't read walls of text
- Mention time to set up: **"15 minutes"** sounds manageable
- Offer to call if they prefer – some prefer voice
- Follow up consistently – most pilots come after 2-3 touches
