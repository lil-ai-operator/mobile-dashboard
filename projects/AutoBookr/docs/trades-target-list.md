# Trades Target List

Targeted UK trades for AutoBookr pilot outreach.

---

## 1. Plumbers

**Why target:** High volume of reactive call-outs, customer no-shows waste significant time, typically sole traders or small teams without admin support.

**Pain points:**
- No-shows losing them £80-200+ per job
- Chasing payments from domestic customers
- Booking admin eats into actual plumbing time
- Missed calls = lost jobs

**Contact angle:** "Never miss a booking again. AutoBookr confirms appointments automatically and collects payment upfront—so you turn up to jobs that actually show up."

---

## 2. Electricians

**Why target:** Similar to plumbers—high no-show rates, Part P compliance means paperwork matters, often working solo on domestic jobs.

**Pain points:**
- No-shows especially painful when they've blocked out half a day
- Customer uncertainty about timing (can't leave customer waiting while doing another job)
- Test certificates and paperwork admin
- Competitive market—reputation depends on reliability

**Contact angle:** "Every missed appointment costs you a full day's earnings. AutoBookr handles the booking confirmations so you only turn up to jobs that are guaranteed."

---

## 3. Roofers

**Why target:** Weather-dependent—jobs get cancelled last minute, large jobs require deposits, high-value contracts need professional booking.

**Pain points:**
- Weather cancellations leaving gaps in schedule
- Customers ghosting after quote
- Large deposits difficult to collect
- Seasonal crunch—need to maximise available days

**Contact angle:** "Weather kills your schedule. AutoBookr fills gaps automatically, confirms with customers 24hrs before, and collects deposits to stop no-shows."

---

## 4. Gas Engineers / Heating Engineers

**Why target:** Highly regulated, tight scheduling windows, emergency call-outs mixed with planned maintenance, excellent fit for automated booking.

**Pain points:**
- Emergency vs. scheduled job conflicts
- OFGEM/Gas Safe paperwork demands
- Customer no-shows waste limited appointment slots
- Long working hours—admin time is precious

**Contact angle:** "Your time is too valuable to spend chasing bookings. AutoBookr confirms every appointment and handles the back-and-forth—so you focus on the actual work."

---

## 5. Carpenters & Joiners

**Why target:** Project-based work, material lead times, often sub-contracting or small firms. Good for both domestic and commercial.

**Pain points:**
- Customer changes to scope/spec after starting
- Material delivery delays throwing off schedule
- Deposit collection for larger projects
- Rework due to miscommunication

**Contact angle:** "Avoid the miscommunication that leads to rework. AutoBookb keeps every appointment confirmed and documented—so both sides know exactly what's agreed."

---

## 6. Painters & Decorators

**Why target:** High volume of small domestic jobs, often single-person trades, very price-sensitive customers who ghost on quotes.

**Pain points:**
- Customers getting multiple quotes then going silent
- Day-rate work makes no-shows directly costly
- Prep time (moving furniture, etc.) wasted if job cancelled
- Low margins—can't afford wasted days

**Contact angle:** "Every no-show is a day without earnings. AutoBookr confirms appointments and collects commitment—so you don't turn up to find no one's in."
