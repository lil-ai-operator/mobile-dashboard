## Logo

### Primary Logo (Concept A - The Hook)
- **Concept**: Phone icon with curved hook shape
- **Color**: Deep Blue #1E40AF
- **Use**: Website header, app icon, all primary contexts
- **File**: `logo-concept-a.png`

### Secondary Logo (Concept B - The Checkmark)
- **Concept**: Phone with emerald checkmark badge
- **Color**: Emerald Green #22C55E
- **Use**: Success states, marketing, dark backgrounds
- **File**: `logo-concept-b.png`

### Logo Clear Space
- Minimum clear space: Height of the "A" letter around all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or change colors

## UI Components (from Dashboard Mockup)

### Button Styles
- **Primary**: Background #1E40AF, white text, 8px border-radius
- **Secondary**: Border #3B82F6, transparent background
- **Success**: Background #22C55E

### Card Style
- Background: #1E293B

- Border-radius: 12px
- Padding: 16-24px
