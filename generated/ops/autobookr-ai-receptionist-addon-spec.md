# AutoBookr AI Receptionist Add-On Spec (Phase 2)

## Product positioning
Start with instant missed-call text-back. Upgrade to AI receptionist when you’re ready for full call handling.

## Add-on name
AutoBookr AI Receptionist

## Feature list
1. 24/7 call answering with natural voice assistant
2. Lead qualification (job type, postcode, urgency, budget range)
3. Appointment booking into calendar slots
4. Smart call routing (urgent calls to owner)
5. Missed-call fallback to instant SMS
6. FAQ handling (hours, service area, pricing-from)
7. Call summary + transcript to dashboard
8. Follow-up automation (quote reminder, re-engagement)

## Commercial packaging (recommended)
- Growth add-on: AI receptionist lite
- Pro include: AI receptionist full + routing + transcripts
