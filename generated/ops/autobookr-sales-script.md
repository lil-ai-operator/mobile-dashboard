# AutoBookr Sales Script - Pilot Calls

## Friendly, Natural Sales Conversation Guide

---

### 1. Opening Hook

**Goal:** Get attention in the first 10 seconds

> "Hi [Name], it's [Your Name] from AutoBookr. Hope you're having a good [day/week]! I noticed you runs a [trade/business type] and I wanted to chat briefly — we've got a tool that's been helping tradespeople in [their area] never miss another booking. Have you got 2 minutes?"

**Alternative if they seem busy:**
> "No problem at all — I'll be quick. Basically, we help tradespeople automatically capture every call and booking, even when they're on a job or can't answer. Sound useful?"

---

### 2. Value Proposition

**Goal:** Connect the problem to their wallet

> "Here's the thing — we looked at the data, and the average tradesperson misses about 3-5 calls a week. That's potentially £100-£200 in lost jobs right there. And most of the time, it's not because they don't want the work — they're just on a tool or with a customer and can't pick up."

**Pain points to mention:**
- Calls missed while on another job
- Voicemails never checked
- Customers going to competitors
- Admin time spent chasing quotes
- Jobs booked but details lost

> "With AutoBookr, every call gets answered, every job gets booked, and you don't lift a finger. It basically acts as your virtual receptionist 24/7."

---

### 3. How It Works

**Keep it simple — 3 sentences max**

> "It's dead simple. When someone calls, our AI answers, asks what they need, books the job straight into your calendar, and sends you the details. You get a notification with the job info — job done. No apps to install, nothing complicated."

**If they ask for:**
> "It more detail works with your existing phone number — callers won't know they're talking to AI. It can handle booking questions, pricing queries, availability checks — all the stuff that eats up your time."

---

### 4. Pricing

**Goal:** Position correctly, lead with value

> "We've got three tiers to fit different needs:
>
> - **Starter at £19/month** — just the essentials: call answering, booking capture, SMS confirmations. Perfect if you're solo and just need help not missing calls.
> - **Growth at £49/month** — adds things like CRM integration, job dispatch, and customer follow-ups. Good if you've got a small team.
> - **Pro at £99/month** — full package: multiple lines, priority support, analytics, and we can even handle payments and invoicing."

**If price comes up early:**
> "Think of it this way — if it books you just one extra job a month, it's already paid for itself. Most people see that within the first week."

---

### 5. Handling Objections

#### "I'm too busy / don't have time"

> "Totally get it — that's actually exactly why people love it. You don't need to do anything. We handle it all. You just show up to the jobs we book for you. 5 minutes to set up, then zero ongoing effort."

#### "Not interested"

> "Fair enough — I appreciate you being straight with me. Just so you know, there's no commitment to try it. We do a free pilot for 14 days — you see if it works for you, and if not, no worries. Worth a quick look?"

#### "I need to think about it / talk to my partner"

> "Absolutely, makes sense. Here's what I'll do — I'll send you a quick overview and we can schedule a 10-minute demo whenever works for you. No pressure at all. What email shall I send it to?"

#### "My current system works fine"

> "That's great — I'm not here to fix what isn't broken. But if you're ever curious what you might be missing — calls we could capture, jobs you didn't see — we're here. Happy to do a free trial so you can compare with zero risk."

#### "It's too expensive"

> "I hear you. But think about it this way — what's one lost job a week worth to you? That's £150-£200 minimum. This pays for itself after the first couple of jobs. And if it doesn't deliver, you're not stuck — month-to-month, cancel anytime."

---

### 6. Closing for Trial/Pilot

**Soft close:**
> "Would it be helpful to try it free for 14 days? No card needed, just see how it works with your real calls. If you love it, great. If not, just let us know and we wrap it up. No hassle."

**If they hesitate:**
> "How about this — let me set you up with the Starter plan for free, and you can test it for a week. If you get even one booking from it, you're ahead. Deal?"

**Next steps:**
> "Perfect — I'll get your setup now. You'll get a quick email to confirm everything, and you're live. I'll check in after 3 days to see how it's going. Sound good?"

**Remember:** Be friendly, be brief, and listen more than you talk. The goal isn't to sell — it's to see if they're a good fit. If they are, the product sells itself.
