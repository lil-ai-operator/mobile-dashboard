# AutoBookr - Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. How does it work?
AutoBookr answers your phone lines 24/7 and books jobs straight into your calendar. When customers call, our AI greets them professionally, collects the job details, checks your availability, and confirms the booking — no manual effort from you.

## 2. Do I need any equipment?
Nope! Just a phone line and a smartphone or computer to manage your settings and view bookings. Everything runs in the cloud — no fancy hardware or installations needed.

## 3. What if I miss a call?
No worries — AutoBookr never misses. Every call gets answered, even at 3am. Job details are captured and sent straight to you, so you'll never lose a lead because you were unavailable.

## 4. Can I customize the voicemail?
Absolutely! You can personalize your greeting, set business hours, define your services and pricing, and even add specific questions we should ask callers. It's fully tailored to your trade and brand.

## 5. Is it really 24/7?
Yes — that's the whole point. Whether it's a Sunday evening, a bank holiday, or the middle of the night, AutoBookr is always on to capture every opportunity.

## 6. How do I get started?
Just sign up, set up your business details (services, hours, calendar), and point your phone line to AutoBookr. We handle the rest — typically up and running within minutes.

## 7. What trades is it for?
AutoBookr works for any trade that takes bookings: plumbers, electricians, locksmiths, HVAC, gardeners, cleaners, painters, builders, mechanics, taxis, beauty therapists, and more.

## 8. Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime with one click. You're in control.
