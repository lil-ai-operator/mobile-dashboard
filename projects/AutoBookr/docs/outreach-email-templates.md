## Tips for Use

- **Personalise:** Always replace [placeholders] with real data. "Plumber in Manchester" lands better than generic.
- **Add local context:** Mention a nearby town or trade shows you've attended in their region.
- **Follow up:** Use the follow-up sequence—first email is just the hook.
- **Keep it short:** Tradespeople skim. 3-4 short paragraphs max.
- **Mobile-first:** Many will read on their phone. Short sentences, big buttons (if HTML), clear CTAs.
