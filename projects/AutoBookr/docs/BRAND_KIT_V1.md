# AutoBookr Brand Kit v1

## Overview
Professional, trustworthy, UK-trade-focused brand identity for AutoBookr.

---

## Logo Concepts

### Concept A: "The Hook" (Primary)
- **Visual**: Simple phone icon with a curved "hook" shape suggesting connection/callback
- **Vibe**: Trustworthy, immediate action, professional
- **Colors**: Primary blue (#1E40AF) + accent green (#22C55E)
- **Use case**: App icon, header logo, favicon

### Concept B: "The Checkmark"
- **Visual**: Phone silhouette with a checkmark badge overlay
- **Vibe**: Reliability, completed calls, guaranteed response
- **Colors**: Primary green (#22C55E) + dark slate (#1E293B)
- **Use case**: Marketing materials, success indicators

### Concept C: "The Wave"
- **Visual**: Abstract signal/wave pattern forming a subtle phone shape
- **Vibe**: Modern, tech-forward, innovative
- **Colors**: Gradient blue to teal (#1E40AF → #14B8A6)
- **Use case**: Landing page hero, presentations

## Typography

### Headings
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights**: 700 (Bold)
- **Sizes**: 
  - H1: 48px / 3rem
  - H2: 36px / 2.25rem
  - H3: 24px / 1.5rem

### Body
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights**: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- **Sizes**:
  - Large: 18px / 1.125rem
  - Base: 16px / 1rem
  - Small: 14px / 0.875rem

### Monospace (for codes/IDs)
- **Font**: JetBrains Mono
- **Weight**: 400

## Icon Style

### Rules
- **Style**: Outlined (not filled) - consistent stroke width 2px
- **Corners**: Rounded (4px radius)
- **Size**: 24px standard, 20px for tight spaces
- **Color**: Inherit from parent text color

### Key Icons
- Phone/Mobile
- Message/SMS
- Checkmark circle
- Clock/Time
- User/Person
- Settings/Gear
- Chart/Analytics
- Bell/Notification

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Trade-focused (speak "builder", not "user")
- No jargon - plain English
- Action-oriented

### Example Phrases
- "Never miss a lead again"
- "Your virtual receptionist, 24/7"
- "Text back instantly, every time"
- "One recovered job pays for a year"

### Do's and Don'ts
| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use "you" and "your" | Use "the user" or "the customer" |
| Keep sentences short | Use technical jargon |
| Focus on results | Focus on features |
| Be confident | Be overly salesy |
