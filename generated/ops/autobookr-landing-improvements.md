# AutoBookr Landing Page Improvements

## Summary
The current landing page is well-structured with good content. These recommendations focus on strengthening the hero, enhancing trust signals, clarifying pricing, and improving CTAs for better conversion.

---

## 1. Hero Section

### Current:
> **Never miss a booking again**
> 
> AutoBookr instantly texts back missed callers with a booking link — so you stop losing leads while you're on the tools.

### Recommended:
> **Stop Losing Customers While You're on the Tools**
> 
> AutoBookr texts customers back instantly when you miss their call — with a booking link they can tap to book immediately. No more lost leads. No more phone tag. Just more jobs.
> 
> **Start Free Trial** or **See How It Works**

### Why:
- More specific pain point (on the tools = relatable for trades)
- Outcome-focused (stop losing, more jobs)
- Two clear action options

---

## 2. Trust Signals

### Current Trust Strip:
- 🇬🇧 UK Phone Numbers
- 🔐 UK PSDM Compliant
- ⚡ Replies in seconds
- 🔒 Secure Stripe checkout

### Recommended Additions:
Add below the trust strip or in hero:

- **"500+ UK Trade Businesses"** or actual count
- **"10,000+ Leads Recovered"** or actual count
- **"4.9/5 Stars"** (if available)
- **"Setup in 24 Hours"** (more specific than "live in under 24 hours")
- **"No Credit Card Required to Start"** or similar risk-reducer

### Trust Badges to Add (visual):
- Trustpilot / Google Reviews logo placeholder
- ISO 27001 certification badge (if applicable)
- "Used by" logo strip with trade business types

---

## 3. Pricing Display

### Current:
Clear 3-tier pricing but could improve clarity on value.

### Recommended Changes:

**Starter (£19/mo)**
- Tagline: "Perfect for solo traders"
- Add: "Recover up to 50 missed calls/month"

**Growth (£49/mo)** 
- Tagline: "Most popular choice"
- Highlight: "⭐ Best Value" badge
- Add: "Unlimited missed calls"

**Pro (£99/mo)**
- Tagline: "For growing teams"
- Add: "Multi-location support"

**Add to all plans:**
- "Cancel anytime — no penalties"
- "14-day money-back guarantee"
- Visual checkmarks for included features

### Pricing CTA buttons:
- Current: "Choose [Plan Name]"
- Recommended: "Start Free 14-Day Trial" or "Get Started"

---

## 4. Call-to-Action Improvements

### Primary CTAs:
| Section | Current | Recommended |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Hero | "Start Recovering Leads" | "Start Free Trial" or "Get Started — No Credit Card" |
| Pricing | "Choose [Plan]" | "Start Free Trial" |
| Throughout | Various | Add secondary CTA: "Book a Demo" |

### Button Styling:
- Make primary CTAs more prominent (larger, brighter colour)
- Add subtle animation (hover effect)
- Ensure contrast ratio meets accessibility standards

---

## 5. Additional Copy Improvements

### ROI Section:
- Add a simple formula: "1 Missed Call = ~£350 Lost Job"
- Add: "Average AutoBookr customer recovers 20+ missed calls/month"

### Results Section:
- Add: "Based on [X] month average" or similar to ground the numbers

### How It Works:
- Consider adding icons/graphics for each step
- Make step 3 clearer: "You get the lead" → "You get notified and the job is booked"

### FAQ:
- Good but could add: "What happens if I'm already using [competitor]?"
