# AutoBookr Logo Brief v1

## Objective
Create a trust-first logo that feels practical and modern for UK trade businesses.

## Concept directions
1. Phone + lightning bolt (instant response)
2. Phone + checkmark (lead recovered)
3. Chat bubble + wrench (trade-specific automation)

## Wordmark
AutoBookr (emphasize "Bookr" with primary color)

## Usage pack needed
- Full logo (horizontal)
- Icon-only mark
- Light and dark variants
- Favicon (32x32)

## Constraints
- Must be legible at small sizes
- Avoid over-complex gradients
- Works on dark nav and light hero backgrounds
