## 1. Blog Post Outline: "Why Trades Miss Calls and How to Stop It"

### Hook / Introduction
- The cost of missed calls in the trades industry (real numbers)
- The paradox: tradespeople arebusy BUT need more customers
- What you'll learn: practical solutions, not just problems

### Section 1: Why Trades Miss Calls (The Root Causes)
- **On the job, literally** – Can't answer when hands are dirty
- **No out-of-hours coverage** – Calls at 7pm = voicemail = lost customer
- **Phone anxiety** – "Who is this?" → ignore → regret
- **No system** – Relying on memory = relying on failure

### Section 2: The Customer Journey Break
- When someone needs a plumber/electrician NOW
- The 15-minute window: if you don't answer, they're already calling your competitor
- Real story: "I called 3 plumbers. First one answered. Job done."

### Section 3: The Solution – Smart Call Handling
- **After-hours voicemail with fast response** – Set expectations, capture the lead
- **Call routing** – Route to a colleague or on-call team
- **SMS follow-up** – "Can't answer now, can I help tomorrow?" = still in the game
- **Booking integration** – Let them book themselves (AutoBookr magic)

### Section 4: What Trades Lose (The Math)
- 10 missed calls/week × 52 weeks × £40 avg job = £20,800/year gone
- Multiply by 5 years = over £100k in lost revenue
- The fix costs a fraction of that

### Section 5: Action Steps
- Audit your missed calls today
- Set up after-hours routing
- Add SMS automation
- Track every lead

### CTA
- "Want to see exactly how much you're losing? Book a call."
