# AutoBookr Demo Video Script

**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

---

## 1. HOOK (3-5 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Dark screen with phone ringing, caller ID shows "Unknown." Call goes to voicemail. Customer shakes head and walks away.

**AUDIO (VO):**
> "Ever missed a call... and lost a customer?"

**TEXT ON SCREEN:** [Customer Lost 🔴]

---

## 2. PROBLEM (5 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Split screen showing:
- Left: Voicemail inbox overflowing
- Right: Business owner looking frustrated, phone in hand

**AUDIO (VO):**
> "Every missed call is money walking out the door. You're busy, you can't answer every time—but neither can your competitors."

**TEXT ON SCREEN:** [Missed calls = Missed revenue]

---

## 3. SOLUTION (10 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Animated phone screen showing AutoBookr interface. Phone rings → AutoBookr answers instantly → Booking confirmed.

**AUDIO (VO):**
> "AutoBookr is your 24/7 booking assistant. It answers every call, books appointments, and confirms everything—no matter the hour."

**TEXT ON SCREEN:**
- 24/7 Availability
- Instant Booking
- Auto-Confirmation

---

## 4. DEMO (20 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Screen recording-style flow:

| Scene | Visual | Audio |
|-------|--------|-------|
| 4.1 | Customer calls business number | Phone rings |
| 4.2 | AutoBookr picks up: "Hi, thanks for calling! Would you like to book an appointment?" | AutoBookr voice |
| 4.3 | Customer says "Yes, Thursday at 2pm" | Speech bubble appears |
| 4.4 | AutoBookr: "Great! Thursday the 15th at 2pm. Should I confirm that for you?" | Confirmation prompt |
| 4.5 | Customer says "Yes" | Speech bubble |
| 4.6 | Screen shows: ✅ Booking Confirmed + Calendar update + SMS sent | Success sound |
| 4.7 | Business owner gets notification: "New booking: Thursday 2pm" | Notification chime |

**TEXT ON SCREEN:** [Live demo: See it in action]

---

## 5. CTA (5 seconds)

**VISUAL:** AutoBookr logo + website URL. Happy business owner looking at calendar.

**AUDIO (VO):**
> "Never miss another booking. Get AutoBookr today."

**TEXT ON SCREEN:**
- **autobookr.com**
- [Start Free Trial button]
